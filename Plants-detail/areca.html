Plant Details


The Areca Palm (Dypsis lutescens), also known as the Butterfly Palm or Golden Cane Palm, is a graceful, feathery-leafed plant native to Madagascar. Its clumping fronds with arching green leaflets make it a favorite choice for homes, offices, and indoor gardens seeking a tropical touch.

Thriving best in bright, indirect light, Areca Palm prefers well-draining, fertile soil that remains lightly moist but never soggy. It enjoys warm, humid environments and is especially effective at naturally humidifying indoor air, making it great for rooms with AC or dry air.

This palm is non-toxic to pets and children, making it a safe indoor option. It also made NASAâ€™s list of top air-purifying plants, helping filter out indoor pollutants.

Though low-maintenance, it benefits from regular cleaning and attention to watering needs, as overwatering or underwatering can cause brown tips.
